# Why Is My Heating Not Turning On? (Complete Troubleshooting Guide)

A heating system that won’t turn on is one of the most stressful household problems, especially in cold weather. The good news is that in most cases the issue is **not a broken boiler**, but a simple problem like settings, pressure, power supply, or safety lockouts.

This guide walks through every possible cause in a structured way so you can quickly identify the problem and restore heating safely.

---

## 🔌 1. No power to the heating system

![Boiler control panel and power switch](/images/heating-not-turning-on/heating-1-power.jpg "Boiler control panel showing power switch and display")

### Symptoms
- Boiler display is off completely
- No lights or response from system
- Thermostat does nothing

### Common causes
- Tripped fuse or breaker
- Plug switched off (for plug-in boilers)
- Power outage
- Faulty wiring or isolator switch

### Fix
- Check fuse box / circuit breaker
- Reset tripped switch
- Ensure boiler isolator switch is ON
- Confirm house has electricity

💡 If other appliances also don’t work, it’s likely a general electrical issue.

---

## 🌡️ 2. Thermostat is set incorrectly

![Home thermostat control](/images/heating-not-turning-on/heating-2-thermostat.jpg "Room thermostat used to control heating system")

### Symptoms
- Boiler is on but heating does not start
- Room temperature feels low
- System responds inconsistently

### Fix
- Set thermostat to at least 21°C
- Switch heating mode ON (not just hot water)
- Replace thermostat batteries if needed
- Check smart thermostat connection (Wi-Fi/app)

---

## 🔥 3. Boiler pressure too low

![Boiler pressure gauge low reading](/images/heating-not-turning-on/heating-3-pressure.jpg "Boiler pressure gauge showing low system pressure")

### Symptoms
- Heating system fails to start
- Boiler shows error code
- Radiators remain cold

### Ideal pressure
- Normal range: **1.0 – 1.5 bar**
- Too low: below **1.0 bar**

### Fix
- Check pressure gauge
- Use filling loop to top up system
- Restore to ~1.2 bar
- Restart boiler

---

## 🚿 4. Airlock in the system

![Air in heating system pipes](/images/heating-not-turning-on/heating-4-airlock.jpg "Air trapped in heating pipes blocking water flow")

### Symptoms
- Boiler runs but radiators stay cold
- Gurgling sounds in pipes
- Uneven heating or no circulation

### Fix
- Bleed radiators one by one
- Start with highest floor radiators
- Check pressure after bleeding
- Restart heating system

---

## ⚠️ 5. Boiler lockout (safety shutdown)

![Boiler error display screen](/images/heating-not-turning-on/heating-5-error.jpg "Boiler display showing fault or error code")

### Symptoms
- Boiler shows error code
- Heating does not start at all
- Reset button required

### Why it happens
- Overheating
- Pressure drop
- Internal fault detected
- Ignition failure

### Fix
- Press reset button once
- Wait 5–10 minutes
- If error returns → note code
- Do not repeatedly reset

---

## 🔁 6. Timer or schedule issue

![Heating timer control panel](/images/heating-not-turning-on/heating-6-timer.jpg "Heating timer system controlling on/off schedule")

### Symptoms
- Heating works only at certain times
- System appears “dead” but is scheduled off

### Fix
- Check timer settings
- Set heating to “ON” or “Continuous”
- Review smart schedule settings
- Disable eco or away mode temporarily

---

## 🧱 7. Pump or circulation failure (advanced issue)

![Heating pump system component](/images/heating-not-turning-on/heating-7-pump.jpg "Central heating circulation pump inside boiler system")

### Symptoms
- Boiler heats but radiators stay cold
- No water movement in system
- Uneven heat distribution

### Fix
- Tap pump gently (if stuck)
- Check for air in pump
- Professional inspection may be required

---

## 🧯 8. Gas supply issue (if applicable)

![Gas boiler supply valve](/images/heating-not-turning-on/heating-8-gas.jpg "Gas supply valve connected to boiler system")

### Symptoms
- Boiler attempts to start but fails
- No flame ignition
- Error codes related to gas supply

### Fix
- Check gas valve is open
- Confirm other gas appliances work
- Contact gas supplier if outage suspected

---

## 🔎 Quick diagnosis checklist

If your heating is not turning on:

- No lights? → Power issue
- Boiler on but no heat? → Thermostat issue
- Low pressure? → Refill system
- Error code? → Boiler lockout
- Random schedule? → Timer issue
- Partial heating failure? → Airlock or pump issue

---

## ⚡ Step-by-step full recovery checklist

- Check electricity supply
- Check thermostat settings
- Verify boiler pressure
- Reset boiler once
- Bleed radiators if needed
- Check timer/schedule
- Confirm gas supply
- Wait 10–15 minutes for system stabilization

---

## ❌ What NOT to do

- Do NOT keep resetting boiler repeatedly
- Do NOT ignore repeated pressure drops
- Do NOT open internal boiler components
- Do NOT bypass safety systems

---

## 🛠️ Prevention tips

- Check pressure monthly
- Service boiler yearly
- Replace thermostat batteries annually
- Bleed radiators each heating season
- Keep vents and boiler area clear

## ❓ People also ask (SEO FAQ section)

### Why is my heating on but radiators cold?
This is usually caused by low pressure, air in the system, or circulation pump issues.

### Why does my boiler keep turning off?
It may be due to low pressure, overheating, or a fault requiring reset.

### Why is my heating not responding to thermostat?
Thermostat settings, batteries, or connectivity issues are the most common causes.

---

## 📌 Summary

Most heating systems that won’t turn on are caused by:
- power or electrical issues
- incorrect thermostat settings
- low boiler pressure
- air in the system
- boiler safety lockout

In most cases, the problem can be resolved in under 20 minutes without professional help.
