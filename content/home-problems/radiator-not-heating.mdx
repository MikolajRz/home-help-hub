# Why Is My Radiator Not Heating Properly?

A radiator that is not heating is usually caused by air in the system or low pressure.

## Common causes

- Air trapped inside radiator
- Low boiler pressure
- Closed valve

## Solution

Bleed the radiator and check boiler pressure.
