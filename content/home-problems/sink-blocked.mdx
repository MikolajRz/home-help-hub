# Blocked Sink: Causes, Fixes & Prevention Guide

A blocked sink is one of the most common household plumbing issues. In most cases, it is caused by a gradual buildup of **grease, food debris, soap residue, or hair**, rather than a sudden failure.

This guide explains how to diagnose the blockage, remove it safely, and prevent it from coming back.

---

## ⚠️ 1. How to recognize a blocked sink

![Blocked kitchen sink water not draining](/images/sink-blocked/sink-1-overflow.jpg "Kitchen sink draining very slowly due to blockage")

### Common symptoms
- Water drains very slowly or not at all
- Gurgling sounds from the drain
- Bad smell coming from sink
- Water backing up into basin

👉 The severity depends on whether the blockage is partial or complete.

---

## 🍳 2. Grease and fat buildup (most common cause)

![Grease buildup in sink pipe](/images/sink-blocked/sink-2-grease.jpg "Fat and grease solidifying inside kitchen drain pipe")

### Why it happens
- Hot oil poured into sink
- Cooking fats washed down drain
- Grease solidifies inside pipes over time

### Symptoms
- Gradual slowing of drainage
- Worse after washing dishes
- Strong unpleasant smell

### Fix
- Pour boiling water slowly down drain
- Use dish soap + hot water flush
- Apply baking soda + vinegar mixture
- Repeat until flow improves

💡 Prevention is more important than repair here.

---

## 🧼 3. Soap and detergent residue buildup

![Soap scum blocking drain pipe](/images/sink-blocked/sink-3-soap.jpg "Soap residue accumulating inside plumbing pipe")

### Why it happens
- Soap mixes with minerals in water
- Creates sticky layer inside pipes
- Catches other debris over time

### Symptoms
- Slow drainage even with clean sink
- White or grey residue in drain area

### Fix
- Use vinegar-based flush
- Mechanical drain snake for stubborn buildup
- Regular hot water flushing

---

## 🍝 4. Food debris and solid waste blockage

![Food debris clogging sink drain](/images/sink-blocked/sink-4-food.jpg "Food particles blocking kitchen sink drain pipe")

### Common causes
- Rice, pasta, coffee grounds
- Vegetable scraps
- Small food particles stuck in trap

### Symptoms
- Sudden blockage after cooking
- Water rising quickly in sink
- Complete drainage stop

### Fix
- Remove sink trap (U-bend) and clean manually
- Use drain snake or wire tool
- Flush system with hot water after cleaning

---

## 🧻 5. Hair blockage (bathroom sinks)

![Hair clog inside bathroom sink drain](/images/sink-blocked/sink-5-hair.jpg "Hair accumulation inside bathroom sink drain causing blockage")

### Why it happens
- Hair mixes with soap and forms clumps
- Builds up slowly over time

### Symptoms
- Slow bathroom sink drainage
- Visible hair near drain
- Frequent recurring blockages

### Fix
- Use drain hook or hair removal tool
- Clean trap regularly
- Chemical drain cleaner (carefully)

---

## 🧱 6. Deep pipe blockage (advanced issue)

![Deep blockage inside pipe system](/images/sink-blocked/sink-6-deep.jpg "Severe blockage deeper in plumbing system pipe")

### Symptoms
- No improvement after cleaning trap
- Multiple sinks affected
- Water backing up heavily

### Fix
- Drain snake (long cable tool)
- Professional plumbing jet cleaning
- Inspect main drainage line if needed

---

## 🔎 Step-by-step diagnosis checklist

Follow this order:

1. Is water completely blocked? → deep blockage
2. Is only one sink affected? → local pipe issue
3. Does smell occur? → grease or food buildup
4. Is bathroom sink affected? → hair blockage likely

---

## ⚠️ What NOT to do

- Do NOT pour oil or grease into sink
- Do NOT overuse chemical drain cleaners
- Do NOT ignore slow drainage (it gets worse over time)
- Do NOT force water if completely blocked

---

## 🛠️ Prevention tips

- Never pour grease into sink
- Use sink strainers
- Flush with hot water weekly
- Clean U-bend monthly
- Avoid washing large food scraps into drain

---

## ❓ People also ask (SEO FAQ section)

### Why is my sink draining slowly?
Most likely caused by grease or soap buildup inside pipes.

### Can boiling water unclog a sink?
Yes, for light grease blockages it often helps.

### When should I call a plumber?
If multiple drains are blocked or water backs up heavily.

---

## 📌 Summary

A blocked sink is usually caused by:
- grease and fat buildup
- soap residue accumulation
- food particles in pipes
- hair blockage (bathrooms)
- deep pipe obstruction in severe cases

Most sink blockages can be fixed at home using simple tools like hot water, vinegar solutions, or a drain snake.
